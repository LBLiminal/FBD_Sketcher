Point tools: single click to place. Beams, loads & dimensions: drag, or click-move-click. Drag the square handles to edit geometry; drag the round handle to move an element's label clear of overlaps (Reset label position in properties puts it back). Loads and supports placed on a beam attach to it and follow when it moves — drag them clear to detach, or use Detach in properties. In engineer mode the equilibrium panel can Solve reactions for a determinate support set (pin + roller, or one fixed), and grid snapping switches to real units. Diagrams autosave to this browser; use Save for a portable .json.

Clear the canvas?

This removes everything on the canvas. You can still undo with Ctrl+Z.
